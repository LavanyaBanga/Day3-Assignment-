Storyline

Set in a surreal lavender-toned dreamscape, *Purple Dreams* follows the journey of Arya, a young girl seeking answers about the fading colors in her world. As she meets strange yet wise dream-beings, Arya uncovers the truth about creativity, love, and self-acceptance. A film that touches the soul with emotion, visuals, and sound.
